## Key Features & How-To Instructions

### 1. Command Panel & Health Metrics
1. Navigate to `/admin`.
2. View **System Health Diagnostics Widget** (Server status, DB status, API speed, active users).

### 2. Manage Grievances & Assign Field Officers
1. Go to **Complaint Tracker** or **Complaint Inbox**.
2. Click on a complaint to view citizen photos, location map, and details.
3. Select an available Field Officer from Electricity, Water Supply, or Drainage & Waste Management.
4. Set expected completion date and click **Reassign Worker**.

### 3. Verify Completion & Reopen Cases
1. Inspect completion evidence uploaded by field officers.
2. Click **Approve & Close** or **Reopen Complaint**.

### 4. Interactive Analytics & Downloadable Reports
1. Navigate to **Analytics** to view 10 KPI cards, 7 Chart.js interactive charts, geographic heatmaps, and worker leaderboards.
2. Navigate to **Reports** to apply filters (Date range, status, priority, department, worker) and download **PDF**, **Excel**, or **CSV** reports.
