## Key Features & How-To Instructions

### 1. Register & Login
1. Navigate to `/register` or `/login`.
2. Enter your name, email, phone, address, and password.

### 2. Raise a Civic Complaint
1. Click **Raise a Complaint** on the Dashboard header.
2. Select Category (Roads, Water, Electricity, Sanitation, Drainage).
3. Use the interactive map to pinpoint location coordinates.
4. Upload photos of the issue.
5. Click **Submit Complaint**.

### 3. Track Complaint Progress
1. Go to **Complaint Tracker** (`/citizen/tracker`).
2. View real-time status steps: *Submitted → Under Review → Assigned → In Progress → Completed → Closed*.
3. View field officer remarks and completion photos.

### 4. Provide Feedback
1. Once a complaint is marked resolved, navigate to **Feedback**.
2. Submit a 1 to 5 star rating and comments.
