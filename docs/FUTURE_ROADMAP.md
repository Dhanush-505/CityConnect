# CityConnect Future Expansion Roadmap

## Phase 1: Mobile & AI Intelligence
- **React Native Mobile App**: iOS & Android native apps with camera integration and offline offline GPS tagging.
- **AI Auto-Classification**: Automated image recognition to identify potholes, garbage dumps, and water leaks.
- **AI Resolution Time Forecasting**: Machine learning models predicting resolution times based on department workload.

## Phase 2: Multi-Channel Communication & IoT
- **SMS & WhatsApp Alerts**: Automated SMS notifications via Twilio API.
- **IoT Sensor Integration**: Smart water flow sensors and street light fault detectors auto-generating complaints.
- **Multi-City & Multi-Language Support**: Localization in regional languages and multi-tenancy for multiple municipal corporations.
